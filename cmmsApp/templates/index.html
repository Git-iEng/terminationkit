Indoor & Outdoor Termination
Kits

Termination kits are essential electrical accessories used to safely terminate and seal power cables at connection points such as switchgear, transformers, substations, panels, poles, and underground or overhead distribution networks.

They play a critical role in ensuring electrical insulation, mechanical protection, environmental sealing, and effective stress control, thereby enhancing the safety, reliability, and service life of power cable systems across low, medium, and high voltage applications.

Types of Termination Kits

Choose the Right Termination Kit

Indoor Termination Kits

Indoor termination kits are designed for use in controlled environments such as switch rooms, electrical panels, indoor substations, and transformer rooms. These kits offer a compact and space-efficient solution while providing reliable insulation and protection.

Applications

  • Switchgear and control panels
  • Indoor substations
  • Transformer rooms
  • LV, MV and selected HV installations
  • Protection against dust, moisture, and accidental contact

Outdoor Termination Kits

Outdoor termination kits are engineered to perform reliably in harsh and exposed environmental conditions. They are manufactured using UV-resistant, weatherproof, and pollution-resistant materials to withstand rain, sunlight, humidity, temperature variations, and dust.

Applications

  • Overhead distribution networks
  • Underground cable exits
  • Substations and switchyards
  • Pole-mounted and open-yard installations

Our Solutions

Termination Kit Solutions for LV, MV and HV Networks

Indoor Termination Kits

Compact and space-efficient terminations for switch rooms, control panels, indoor substations, and transformer rooms. Designed for reliable insulation, stress control, and safe cable ending in controlled environments.

Outdoor Termination Kits

Weatherproof, UV-resistant and pollution-resistant terminations for exposed installations. Built to withstand rain, sunlight, humidity, dust, and temperature variations for long-term reliability.

Heat Shrink Termination Kits

Proven and widely used solution where heat-shrink tubing provides strong insulation and environmental sealing. Suitable for LV and MV cable terminations with dependable field installation.

Cold Shrink Termination Kits

Fast and safe installation without flame or heating. Excellent sealing against moisture and contamination, ideal for harsh environments and utility applications.

Separable / Plug-in Terminations (RMU / GIS)

Detachable cable terminations for RMU and GIS systems. Enables easier maintenance, safer connections, and improved flexibility for compact substation installations.

Resin / Cast Terminations

Strong mechanical protection and sealing using resin/epoxy compounds. Suitable for industrial environments where robust termination integrity and long service life are required.

Technical Overview

Voltage Range

  • Medium Voltage (MV): 3.3 kV to 33 kV
  • High Voltage (HV): 66 kV and above (on request)

Cable Compatibility

  • Single-core and multi-core cables
  • Copper and aluminium conductors
  • XLPE and EPR insulated cables
  • Armoured and unarmoured constructions
